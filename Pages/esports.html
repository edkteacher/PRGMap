Hybrid Gaming & Education Classroom


Ons nuwe hibriede speletjie- en onderwysklaskamer. Hierdie moderne fasiliteit sal ons eSport-atlete bemagtig om nie net in die mededingende speltoneel uit te blink nie, maar ook in hul opvoedkundige strewes. Hierdie uitrustings, ontwerp vir speel- en opvoeding, sal dien as hulpmiddels vir ons atlete om hul vaardighede te slyp, aan toernooie mee te ding en met trots Paul Roos Gimnasium eSport te verteenwoordig.

Our new hybrid gaming and education classroom. This state-of-the-art facility will empower our eSport athletes to excel not only in the competitive gaming scene but also in their educational pursuits. These rigs, designed for ready-to-play gaming and education, will serve as tools for our athletes to hone their skills, compete in tournaments, and proudly represent Paul Roos Gymnasium eSport.
